Wasabi Japanese Sushi & Grill

Wasabi is a friendly and atmospheric sushi restaurant located in Lake Charles on Ryan Street.

Our expert chefs prepare meals right at the sushi bar, allowing patrons to sit and watch them work their magic.

The quality and craftsmanship of our sushi is attributed to our fresh, flavorful ingredients, coupled with over 2 decades of experience and technique.

Not a fan of sushi? No problem! We have other meals you can enjoy ranging from multiple kinds of hibachi all the way down to delicious grilled chicken yakitori.

Wasabi is a promising dining experience you won't forget.
